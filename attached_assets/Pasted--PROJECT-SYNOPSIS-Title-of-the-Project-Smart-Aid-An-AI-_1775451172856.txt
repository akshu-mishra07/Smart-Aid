📄 PROJECT SYNOPSIS
Title of the Project

Smart Aid: An AI-Powered Web Platform for Poverty Assistance and Resource Accessibility

1. Introduction

Poverty remains one of the most significant challenges in developing countries like India. Although the government provides numerous welfare schemes, subsidies, and employment opportunities, many eligible individuals fail to benefit due to lack of awareness, digital illiteracy, and fragmented information sources.

The proposed system, Smart Aid, is a web-based platform designed to bridge this gap by providing a centralized system where users can easily access information about government schemes, nearby assistance centers, and job opportunities. The platform leverages basic Artificial Intelligence techniques to recommend suitable schemes based on user input.

2. Objective of the Project

The main objectives of the project are:

To develop a user-friendly web application for economically weaker sections
To provide personalized recommendations for government welfare schemes
To help users locate nearby assistance services such as NGOs and hospitals
To provide employment opportunities for daily wage workers
To reduce the information gap and improve accessibility
To promote digital inclusion
3. Problem Statement

Despite various government initiatives, many people face difficulties in accessing benefits due to:

Lack of awareness about schemes
Complex eligibility criteria
No centralized system for information
Limited digital knowledge
Time-consuming manual processes

As a result, many eligible individuals remain deprived of benefits.

4. Proposed Solution

The proposed solution is a centralized web-based platform developed using ASP.NET and SQL Server.

The system allows users to:

Enter personal and economic details
Get recommendations for suitable schemes
Find nearby NGOs, hospitals, and food centers
Search for job opportunities
Upload documents for verification

The system uses rule-based logic to analyze user data and provide accurate recommendations.
